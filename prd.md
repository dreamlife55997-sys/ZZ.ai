# 1. Product Overview

Zz is an AI-powered smartphone control system developed by Elvion Global.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that only respond with information, Zz is designed to interact directly with smartphone functions, applications, and system operations.

The goal of Zz is to create an intelligent control layer between humans and their devices, enabling users to automate tasks, control apps, and manage workflows through AI-driven commands.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Modern smartphones require constant manual interaction across multiple apps and settings.

Users repeatedly perform small tasks such as opening apps, switching between tools, managing notifications, sending messages, or searching information.

This fragmented interaction reduces productivity and creates unnecessary complexity.

Zz aims to simplify this experience by introducing an AI system capable of controlling smartphone functions and automating tasks through intelligent interaction.
